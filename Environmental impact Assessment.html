Environmental Impacts Assessment

We are conducts environmental impacts assessments to identify and mitigate potential environmental effects of development projects.

Our Approach

We use a comprehensive approach to assess environmental impacts, including:

  • Conducting site assessments and field studies
  • Reviewing existing data and literature
  • Consulting with stakeholders and indigenous communities
  • Developing and implementing mitigation measures
