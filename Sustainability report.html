Sustainability Assessment and Reporting

Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our annual sustainability report, which outlines our progress towards achieving our sustainability goals.

Our Sustainability Goals

  • Reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 50% by 2025
  • Increase renewable energy sources to 80% of total energy consumption by 2030
  • Reduce water consumption by 30% by 2025

Our Progress

We have made significant progress towards achieving our sustainability goals, including:

  • Reducing greenhouse gas emissions by 20% in the past year
  • Increasing renewable energy sources to 60% of total energy consumption
  • Reducing water consumption by 15% in the past year
